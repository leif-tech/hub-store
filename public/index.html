FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Reach out on Facebook for anything else.

What does H.U.B stand for?+
H.U.B stands for Hanap.Usap.Build. You Hanap (find) the parts, Usap (talk) with our team about your needs and budget, then we Build your dream PC. It's our whole philosophy in three words.
Do you sell brand new parts or pre-owned?+
We sell both brand new and quality pre-owned components. All pre-owned parts are tested and verified before sale. We have options for every budget.
Can I order online and have it delivered?+
Yes! Place an order through our website and we'll arrange delivery. We serve Valenzuela, Bulacan, Caloocan, and across Metro Manila. Contact us via Facebook or phone to confirm details.
How long does a custom PC build take?+
Most builds are completed same-day or within 24 hours once all parts are available. Custom orders requiring sourced parts may take 2-3 days. We'll give you a timeline during consultation.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept cash, GCash, bank transfer (Metrobank, GoTyme), and online payments. For large orders, we can arrange installment options. Contact us for details.
Can I reserve parts or a build?+
Yes! Message us on Facebook or call 0976 002 1202 to reserve. In-store pickup is available at Arbortowne Plaza. A deposit may be required for custom builds.
Do your builds come with a warranty?+
Yes. Brand new parts carry the manufacturer's warranty. Builds assembled by our team include a labor warranty. Pre-owned parts are tested and sold with a limited warranty. Ask our staff for details.
Can I bring my PC in for repair or upgrade?+
Absolutely. We service walk-in units for diagnosis, repairs, cleaning, and upgrades (RAM, SSD, GPU, etc.). Just bring your unit to our store at Arbortowne Plaza II, Karuhatan, Valenzuela.
What brands do you carry?+
We carry trusted brands including Intel, AMD, NVIDIA, ASUS, MSI, Gigabyte, Kingston, Corsair, Seagate, Western Digital, Samsung, UGREEN, Cooler Master, and more. New stocks arrive regularly so message us for current availability.
Is consultation really free?+
Yes, 100% free. Just tell us your budget and what you'll use the PC for (gaming, school, work, video editing) and we'll recommend the best parts for your money. No obligation to buy.
Do you build PCs for gaming, work, and school?+
Yes. Budget school rigs, mid-range everyday setups, high-end gaming PCs, and professional workstations for video editing or 3D work. Every build is tailored to your needs and budget.
Can I get a parts list and quote before committing?+
Of course. Message us on Facebook with your budget and requirements and we'll send you a full parts list with pricing. No pressure. We want you to be confident before you spend anything.
