Trade-In Estimator Settings

This controls the Trade-In Estimator that customers see on your website.

Conditions — Set how much value a customer gets based on their item's condition (e.g. Excellent = 100%, Good = 70%).

Component Types — Manage the categories customers can choose from (GPU, CPU, RAM, etc.). Each type has models with min/max trade-in values.

Changes take effect on the live site immediately after clicking Save & Publish.
